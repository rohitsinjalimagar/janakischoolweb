Established in 2071 BS, Janaki Montessori School is committed to delivering high-quality early childhood education based on the Montessori philosophy. We create a nurturing environment that promotes independence, creativity, and curiosity, allowing each child to learn and grow at their own pace. Our dedicated and experienced teachers guide students to become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.
